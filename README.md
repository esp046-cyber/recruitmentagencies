# recruitmentagencies
mobile-optimized HTML app for Philippines recruitment agencies specializing in SCADA and I&C engineers with good contrast and readability
professional mobile-optimized HTML app for SCADA and Instrumentation & Control Engineer recruitment in the Philippines!
Key Features:
✅ 10 Recruitment Agencies - Mix of local and international placement agencies
✅ Mobile-Optimized - 1% margins, responsive design, small font titles
✅ High Contrast - Blue gradient background with gold/cyan text for excellent readability
✅ Search Function - Real-time search by agency name, location, or specialization
✅ Filter Tags - Quick filters for SCADA-focused, international, or local agencies
✅ Interactive Cards - Each agency has contact info, specializations, and click-to-call buttons
✅ Practical Guides - Job hunting tips, required documents, and in-demand skills for 2026
✅ Professional Theme - Modern glass-morphism design with 2026 recruitment focus
The app works perfectly on mobile devices and includes agencies specializing in SCADA systems, DCS, PLC programming, industrial automation, and process control engineering positions both locally in the Philippines and internationally (Middle East, Asia Pacific, Europe).
